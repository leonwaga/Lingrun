RO-SY3

High-Capacity Reverse Osmosis Water Filter

Product Details

The RO-SY3 is a high-capacity reverse osmosis water filter designed for both commercial and residential applications. It features advanced filtration technology to remove up to 99.9% of contaminants, including salts, bacteria, particles, sugars, proteins, dyes, and other impurities. This system is suitable for use in restaurants, offices, factories, schools, hotels, and homes, providing clean and safe drinking water.
