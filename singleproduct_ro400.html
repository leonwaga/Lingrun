RO-400

Under The Sink Water Filter

Product Details

RO-400 is a high-capacity, low-cost system that is suitable for commercial and residential applications. It can be used in offices, small factories, manufacturing, restaurants, food processing industries, shopping centers, schools, and hotels.RO-400 systems can remove up to 99.9% of salts and contaminants from different water sources, including municipal, brackish, and surface water. They can block bacteria, particles, sugars, proteins, dyes, and impurities that have a molecular weight of more than 150-250 Dalton.
